Risk flags

Heritage Part IV Designation — Active since 1987

High confidence ▾

This property is designated under the Ontario Heritage Act (Part IV). Any exterior alterations — window replacements, facade repairs, additions — require City Heritage Permit approval. Approvals typically add 6-18 months and $40,000-$60,000 to any exterior renovation budget.

Say this at the table

"The Part IV heritage designation means we're buying a property with permanent renovation constraints. I'd like to reflect the heritage approval premium in the purchase price."

→ Supports $40,000-$60,000 price reduction request

TRCA Flood Plain — 1:100 year event zone
